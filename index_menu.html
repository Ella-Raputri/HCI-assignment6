1. Fried Rice


A MUST TRY in our restaurant!
Cooked rice that has been stir fried in a frying pan, combined with sliced omelet, vegetables, and chicken meat.

2. Chicken Katsu


A Japanese dish consisting a piece of chicken meat, covered in bread crumb, and then fried. Served with some vegetables and mayonnaise as appetizer and sauce to be consumed with the chicken.

Price: Rp 22.000,00


3. Wonton Noodle


Egg noodles served with hot broth, some wonton dumplings, and bok-choy (vegetable). A Chinese dish that combined the soft texture of noodle and thick flavour of hot broth.

Price: Rp 24.000,00


4. Black Pepper Chicken


A dish containing some pieces of chicken meat that are fried with special black pepper sauce and some bell peppes. A combination of the crispy in the outside yet tender in the inside chicken meat with the aroma and the flavour of slightly spicy black pepper sauce.

Price: Rp 22.000,00


5. Bak Kut Teh


A pork dish that consists of pork ribs stewed with a mixture of fragrant herbs, including garlic, cinnamon, star anise, and many more. Delicious tender pork ribs simmered with thick spices broth.

Price: Rp 50.000,00


6. Fried Carrot Cake


A savoury Chinese dish that is made from the mixture of radish (white carrot) and rice flour that is steamed and then fried with eggs and other ingredients. Doesn't consist any carrot. It has two kind of flavours, the sweet black one (fried with sweet black soya sauce) and the appetizing white one (original).

Price: Rp 25.000,00
